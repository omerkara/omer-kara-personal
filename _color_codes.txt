================================================================================
Color Codes for Files
===============
1. Yellow: Important folders/files.

2. Green: CHANGED folders/files.

3. Blue: Taken from someone else and CHANGED folders/files.

4. Purple: Taken from someone else and used AS IS folders/files.

5. Grey: To Check and Change folders/files.

6. Red: To Check/Use and then delete folders/files.

================================================================================
